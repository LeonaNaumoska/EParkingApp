About the application


This application is made in Spring Boot, as a seminar on the subject Web programming. The application is a platform for renting parking spaces in the cities in Macedonia. Users can add their own parking spaces that they will offer for rent, and the places will state in which city they are located, what is the price per hour and a short description of the parking space itself.
